# RoboSense AI

> Building Social Intelligence for Human-Robot Interaction.

---

## Overview

RoboSense AI is a human-centered AI system designed to help robots understand, interpret, and respond to human social contexts.

While traditional robotics focuses on physical capabilities and task execution, RoboSense AI focuses on the social intelligence layer required for robots to collaborate safely and naturally with humans.

The goal is to build robots that are not only capable, but also trustworthy, adaptive, and aligned with human needs.

---

## The Problem

As robots become more integrated into human environments, technical capability alone is not enough.

Robots need to understand:

- Human intentions
- Social context
- Communication patterns
- Personal space
- Emotional signals
- Appropriate interaction behavior

Current robotic systems often struggle with natural human interaction because they lack a comprehensive social intelligence layer.

---

Build a Social Intelligence Engine that enables robots to interact with humans in a safer, more natural, and human-centered way.

## Core Components

## 1. Human Context Understanding

Analyzes human interaction signals:

- Speech patterns
- Body language
- Facial expressions
- Environmental context
- User behavior

---

## 2. Social Intelligence Engine

Provides capabilities including:

- Situation awareness
- Social context interpretation
- Interaction planning
- Appropriate response selection

---

## 3. Human-Robot Care Index (HRCI)

A framework for evaluating robot interaction quality.

Potential dimensions:

- Comfort level
- Trust perception
- Safety awareness
- Communication effectiveness
- Adaptability

---

## 4. Adaptive Interaction Layer

Enables robots to adjust:

- Communication style
- Distance and movement
- Assistance behavior
- Interaction strategy
